# RedeemLoop 100-brand simulation matrix

This document is a non-affiliated simulation design matrix. Brand names and official links are used only as public reference targets for hypothetical RedeemLoop adoption modeling. Do not use real logos, screenshots, product photography, checkout copy, loyalty names, or trademarked campaign assets in the simulator unless the brand has granted permission.

Recommended simulator policy:

- Show a visible "hypothetical simulation" label on every brand scenario.
- Use neutral store mockups, generated product placeholders, and generic campaign names.
- Keep official website and store URLs as outbound references only.
- Recheck each official URL before implementation because regional redirects and ecommerce routes change.

## Shared lifecycle template

Vendor-side flow:

1. Define campaign goal: pickup, prepaid entitlement, loyalty reward, launch access, service credit, experience pass, or bundle redemption.
2. Bind SKU or entitlement to an existing voucher asset.
3. Register merchant vault / receiving address.
4. Publish claim entry on official site, product detail page, membership area, app deep link, POS QR, or livestream page.
5. Accept voucher as tender at checkout, in-store scan, hosted payment page, or customer support-assisted order.
6. Verify receipt and mark commerce order paid / redeemed.
7. Recycle value: reissue, upsell, retarget, partner bundle, renewal credit, or post-redemption loyalty loop.

User-side flow:

1. Discover voucher on brand site, store, email, app, receipt, event page, or packaging QR.
2. Connect wallet or open hosted wallet-free preview.
3. Claim or receive the brand voucher.
4. Use voucher in checkout, store POS, booking flow, or service appointment.
5. See paid / redeemed status and fulfillment instructions.
6. Receive next-loop voucher, upgrade offer, referral pass, or reusable membership proof.

## 可点击仿真体验规范

每个品牌都按同一套仿真模板落地，先做一个品牌的完整闭环，再复制到同一行业的下一个品牌。模板只替换行业语境、商品/服务类型、领取入口、兑换触点和循环投放策略，不改变 RedeemLoop 的核心流程。

| Screen | 厂商视角 | 用户视角 | 仿真目标 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. Campaign setup | 选择行业模板、绑定 SKU/服务、配置券面值、库存、有效期和门店范围。 | 不展示后台，只在官网看到可信的领取入口。 | 证明品牌方可以把券从营销活动变成可核销资产。 |
| 2. Official-site claim | 在官网首页、PDP、会员页、活动页或 App tile 发布领取 CTA。 | 看到品牌风格的活动入口，点击后进入 RedeemLoop 领取页。 | 模拟“品牌官网发券”，不是第三方广告页发券。 |
| 3. Voucher wallet | 查看发行量、已领取量、钱包/免钱包领取模式和风控状态。 | 连接钱包或使用托管预览，确认券规则并领取。 | 降低用户理解成本，让券看起来像订单权益而非复杂链上资产。 |
| 4. Store / checkout redeem | 收银/POS/电商结账/预约系统读取券状态，生成支付或抵扣结果。 | 在店铺、官网结账、预约或客服辅助流程中使用兑换券。 | 证明 RedeemLoop 可以嵌入真实交易触点。 |
| 5. Receipt and loop | 看见核销收据、订单状态、复投规则、下一张券的转化漏斗。 | 获得兑换成功页、履约说明和下一轮权益。 | 展示“兑换 -> 履约 -> 再投放”的循环协议价值。 |

### 单品牌复制方法

以任意一个品牌为样板完成后，同一行业的下一个品牌按下面步骤复用：

1. 保留相同的信息架构：厂商后台、官网领取、用户钱包、店铺核销、循环复投。
2. 替换商品语境：咖啡换成饮品 SKU，美妆换成试用装/正装，酒店换成房晚/升级，汽车换成保养/配件。
3. 替换入口语境：官网 hero、商品详情页、会员页、App 入口、收据 QR、门店海报或直播间挂件。
4. 替换核销触点：Web checkout、POS QR、预约确认、门店柜台、服务工单或数字内容账号。
5. 替换循环策略：加购券、复购券、补货券、升级券、会员券、推荐券、售后服务券。

### 仿真页面建议

建议先做 10 个行业代表品牌的高保真垂直切片，每个切片包含厂商端和用户端两条路径；再把剩余 90 个品牌接入同一套组件和数据表。

| Page | Route idea | 内容 |
|---|---|---|
| Industry index | `/simulations` | 10 个行业入口、100 品牌筛选、生命周期总览。 |
| Brand vendor flow | `/simulations/{industry}/{brand}/merchant` | 发行、渠道、库存、核销、循环投放后台。 |
| Brand official-site mock | `/simulations/{industry}/{brand}/site` | 仿品牌官网/店铺的中性 mock，突出领取 CTA。 |
| Brand user flow | `/simulations/{industry}/{brand}/user` | 用户发现、领取、使用、收据、下一张券。 |
| Scenario analytics | `/simulations/{industry}/{brand}/loop` | 已发行、已领取、已核销、复投转化和库存回流。 |

Implementation note: keep every scenario visibly marked as a hypothetical simulation. Use generated placeholders instead of real logos, screenshots, brand campaigns, product photography, or proprietary loyalty names unless permission is granted.
